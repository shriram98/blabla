Excel

Excel is the annual national level techno-managerial symposium, host by the college. It embraces the right mix of technology and innovation for a contantly evolving community. The fest is renowned for its quality, dynamism and the calibre of its participants.

Excel 2018 will host more than 60 diverse technical, managerial and general events. Including workshops, entrepreneurial summits, hackathons, social innovation competitions, public discussins and much more.

With the firm theme of 'Bridging Realms'. Excel 2018 is all set to unit man and machine, enabling college students to bring together ideation and creation prepping them for the industry.
